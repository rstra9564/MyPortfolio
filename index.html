About Me

I am a competitive dedicated software developer driven by a passion for problem-solving. With three years of hands-on coding experience and ongoing studies toward a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at Boise State University, I specialize in UI design and front-end development, focusing on creating clean, modern, and intuitive interfaces.

My journey in tech began with a curiosity about how things work and has evolved into a commitment to building user-centered, efficient, and well-crafted software. Over the past year, I’ve expanded into full-stack development, gaining experience with SQL databases, Python/Django, and ASP.NET Core. With a strong foundation across both front-end and back-end technologies, I’m dedicated to delivering reliable, impactful solutions and continually advancing my skills.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing knowledge with the developer community.
